Welcome to the home page

This website is dedicated to helping sandwichless people like you learn how to make sandwiches in a fun and easy way! The creators of this site are Corn Dog, Corned Beef, and Corn Flakes.

Click on the images of sandwiches to take you to the sandwich you would like to learn how to make


Corn Dog specializes in making grilled cheese and she will guide you through the grilled cheese making process.

Corned Beef makes super yummy BLT sandwiches so she can teach you how to make BLTs.

Corn Flakes makes the best PB & Js so she will help you to perfect the PB & J making process.
