This page will teach you how to make a super yummy peanut butter and jelly sandwich! Just follow these simple directions and you will be a master at PB & J in no time!

Ingredients

2 tablespoons of jelly
2 tablespoons of peanut butter
2 slices of bread

You will need a knife to spread the ingredients on the bread.

Directions

Get all of the ingredients and knife
Take the knife and spread the peanut butter on a slice of bread
Use the knife to spread the jelly on the other slice
Take the slice of bread with peanut butter and place it on top of the slice with jelly so that the peanut butter and jelly are facing each other
Eat!!!

Fun Facts about PB & J

PB & J stands for peanut butter and jelly
The average American will eat 1500 PB & Js before they turn 18
It was President George W. Bush's favorite sandwich
The largest PB & J ever made was 1,342 pounds. There were 720 pounds of bread, 493 pounds of peanut butter, and 129 pounds of Jelly.
