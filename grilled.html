How to Make a Grilled Cheese

This page will tell you how to make a grilled cheese.




Ingredients

2 pieces of bread
Cheese
Butter

Supplies

Stove
Spatula
pan

Instructions

1. Place desired amount of cheese between the bread slices.
2. Place pan on stove and turn the stove to medium heat.
3. Spead butter on the outside facing sides of the bread, and then place in the pan.
4. Let cook until brown and then flip.
5. Wait until the cheese is melted and then take out of pan.
6. Enjoy!



Fun Facts About Grilled Cheese

The grilled cheese became popular during the Great Depression.
It is a popular sandwich throughout different cultures.
In a grilled cheese contest in 2012 a Japanese man ate 13 grilled cheese's in 60 seconds.
April is known as National Grilled Cheese Sandwich month.
